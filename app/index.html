Sarepta Therapeutics has partnered with award-winning illustrator Mauricio de Sousa to create Cada Passo Importa, the story of Edu, a boy living with Duchenne Muscular Dystrophy. Edu’s story provides a unique perspective on the challenges faced by people living with DMD.

Each comic book contains a story, games, and activities which facilitates understanding and inspires us to look beyond our physical differences.

This comic book will be released in a series. Sign up for updates to be notified of news releases.
